ABOUT CHANNEL

The Channel is an integrated marketing consultancy firm designed to help a brand grow to its full potential. Our wide range of services is customized to provide flexible plan suited for your brand. We help clients understand their brand position in the current market scenario, their competitors and their target audience.

We are a highly experienced team of marketing professionals and creative designers. We work closely with clients to develop a clearly defined proposition and then formulate a clear and concise strategy best suited for their brand. We build your profile and credibility in the market to help your business reach its maximum potential.

The Channel was created by Vandana Kapur, a marketing post-graduate from University of Westminster, UK. Having worked across a number of industries over the past 8 years, Vandana decided to set up a company that would assist businesses develop their marketing strategies.

OUR PROCESS :

We Listen

Before we start to work with you, we listen to your idea, your aspirations and your dreams.

We Plan

We research and gather information and insights into the current market, the competitors and the scope of your idea. We formulate a strategy to develop your idea into reality, build your existing portfolio and add value to your brand.

We Present

We believe it is important to build and cultivate a good relationship with our clients. We appreciate suggestions and criticism to further improvise our strategy.

We Execute

We then execute the plan as per the client’s requirements. Our expert and experienced team deliver innovative & multi-channel approach. We work dedicatedly for each client and we truly believe that our business is to make your business grow.

Once we have delivered as per our commitments, our work continues to ensure you make the most of any future opportunities. We maintain a rapport you have established and focus on the next step to help progress your business to future opportunities.
